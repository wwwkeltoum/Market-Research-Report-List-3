<p><p>The Automotive Ventilated Seats market can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I refers to seats that come with built-in ventilating systems that actively circulate air to keep the driver and passengers cool and comfortable. Type II, on the other hand, involves seats that are equipped with passive ventilation, such as perforated materials that allow air to flow through the seat. Both types aim to enhance the overall driving experience by providing a more pleasant and enjoyable ride.</p></p>
