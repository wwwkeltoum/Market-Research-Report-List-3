<p><p>Air and Gas Leak Detectors Market includes portable detectors, fixed detectors, and detector tubes. Portable detectors are handheld devices used for on-the-go detection of gas leaks. Fixed detectors are stationary devices installed in specific locations to continually monitor gas levels. Detector tubes are glass tubes containing a chemical reagent that changes color in the presence of a particular gas, providing a simple visual indication of gas leaks. These different types cater to various needs and environments for efficient gas leak detection and safety.</p></p>
