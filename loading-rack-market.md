<p><ul><li>Single Pedestal Type</li><li>Tower Support Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Loading rack markets can be categorized into two types: single pedestal type and tower support type. </p><p>Single pedestal type loading racks typically consist of a single support column that holds the loading arms and equipment. They are commonly used in smaller loading areas where space is limited.</p><p>On the other hand, tower support type loading racks feature a larger support structure that includes multiple columns for added stability and support. These types of loading racks are often utilized in larger facilities with higher loading volumes.</p></p>
