<p><p>The Smart Machine Vision System Market includes two main types of camera systems: Line Scan Camera System and Area Scan Camera System. Line Scan Camera Systems are used for inspecting continuous objects moving along a conveyor belt, capturing images line by line to create a complete picture. Area Scan Camera Systems, on the other hand, capture images of still objects in a single shot. Both systems play a vital role in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, automotive, and agriculture for quality control, inspection, and automation processes.</p></p>
