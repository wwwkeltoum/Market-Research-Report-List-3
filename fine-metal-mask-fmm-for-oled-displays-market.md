Etching involves removing material from a metal sheet to create the desired pattern. Electroforming entails depositing metal onto a mold to form the mask. The Multi-material Composite Method combines different materials to achieve precision and durability. Each method offers unique benefits and is used in the production of FMM for OLED displays to meet specific requirements and standards in the industry.</p></p>
