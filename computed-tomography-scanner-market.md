<p><ul><li>Low-slice Scanners (< 64 slices)</li><li>Medium-slice Scanners (64 slices)</li><li>High-slice Scanners (>64 slices)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Computed Tomography (CT) scanners are categorized based on their slice count, which determines the image quality and diagnostic accuracy. Low-slice scanners (<64 slices) are budget-friendly options suitable for basic imaging needs. Medium-slice scanners (64 slices) offer better image resolution and are commonly used for a wide range of diagnostic procedures. High-slice scanners (>64 slices) provide the highest level of detail and are ideal for advanced imaging applications such as cardiac and neurological imaging. Each type caters to different budget and imaging requirements.</p></p>
