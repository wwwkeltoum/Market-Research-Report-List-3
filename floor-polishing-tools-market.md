<p><p>Floor polishing tools market is divided into two main types: dry polishing and wet polishing. Dry polishing involves using tools such as diamond pads and discs with little to no water, resulting in less mess and faster completion times. Wet polishing, on the other hand, utilizes water to cool the polishing pads and reduce dust, resulting in a smoother finish. Both methods have their own advantages and are commonly used in the flooring industry based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
