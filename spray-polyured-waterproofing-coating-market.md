<p><p>Spray polyurea waterproofing coatings can be classified into two main types: pure polyurea coatings and hybrid polyurea coatings. Pure polyurea coatings are made entirely of polyurea resin, offering high durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance. On the other hand, hybrid polyurea coatings combine polyurea with other materials, such as polyurethane, for specific performance enhancements. Both types of coatings are commonly used for waterproofing applications in various industries, providing superior protection against moisture, chemicals, and abrasion.</p></p>
