<p><p>Standalone Automatic Checkweighers are available in different market types, including Electro Magnetic Force Restoration and Strain Gauge Technology. The Electro Magnetic Force Restoration type uses a balance mechanism that restores the balance of the weighing system through magnetic force. On the other hand, Strain Gauge Technology relies on sensors that detect the strain in a load cell to measure weight. Both market types provide accurate and reliable checkweighing solutions for various industries.</p></p>
