<p><p>RF Surface Mount Inductors come in various types, including Wire Wound, Film, and Multilayer. Wire Wound inductors are made of a coil of wire wound around a core, providing high inductance. Film inductors use thin film technology for compact size and high frequency operation. Multilayer inductors have multiple layers of coil wound around a core, offering high inductance in a small package. Each type caters to different application needs in the RF market, providing options for space-saving and high-performance solutions.</p></p>
