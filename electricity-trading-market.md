<p><p>Electricity trading market types include pit trading and over-the-counter (OTC) market. Pit trading involves face-to-face transactions on a trading floor, where traders use hand signals and verbal communication to buy and sell electricity contracts. On the other hand, OTC market is decentralized and transactions occur directly between buyers and sellers. OTC market offers flexibility and customization in contracts, while pit trading provides transparency and price discovery through open outcry trading. Both market types play a crucial role in shaping the electricity trading landscape.</p></p>
