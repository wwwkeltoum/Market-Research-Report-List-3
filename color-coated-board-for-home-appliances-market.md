<p><p>Color coated board for home appliances is divided into two main types: PCM (Pre-coated Metal) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal). PCM is a metal sheet coated with a layer of polymer film, providing a sleek and durable finish. VCM, on the other hand, has a layer of PVC film on top of the metal sheet, offering a wide range of design options and resistance to scratches and abrasions. Both types are widely used in the manufacturing of home appliances for their aesthetic appeal and protective properties.</p></p>
