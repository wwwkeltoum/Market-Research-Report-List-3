<p><p>Dental milling machines come in two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal milling machines typically have a horizontal spindle that holds the cutting tool parallel to the worktable. This type of machine is often used for larger or heavier materials. Vertical milling machines, on the other hand, have a vertical spindle that holds the cutting tool perpendicular to the worktable. These machines are preferred for precision work and are commonly used in dentistry for creating intricate dental prosthetics.</p></p>
