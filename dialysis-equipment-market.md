<p><p>Dialysis Equipment Market is divided into two main types: Hemodialysis Equipment and Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through an artificial kidney machine to remove waste and excess fluids, while Peritoneal Dialysis involves using the lining of the abdomen (peritoneum) as a filter. Both types of equipment are essential for patients with kidney failure to maintain proper blood filtration and fluid balance. The market for these equipments continues to grow as the prevalence of kidney disease increases globally.</p></p>
