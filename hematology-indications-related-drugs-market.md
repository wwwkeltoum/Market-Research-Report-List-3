Cyclooxygenase inhibitors target enzymes involved in prostaglandin synthesis, while antiplatelet agents prevent platelet aggregation. Thrombin inhibitors inhibit the action of thrombin, a key player in the blood clotting process. Demethylating agents are used in the treatment of certain blood disorders. Each of these drug types plays a crucial role in the management of hematologic conditions.</p></p>
