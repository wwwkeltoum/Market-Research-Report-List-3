<p><p>Automatic needle detectors are available in two main types: portable and bench-top. Portable needle detectors are smaller and more compact, making them easy to move around and ideal for smaller workspaces. They are commonly used in industries where products need to be inspected at different locations. Bench-top needle detectors are larger and more stationary, commonly used in factories or production lines where products are systematically inspected. Both types provide accurate and efficient detection of needles or metal contaminants in products, ensuring consumer safety.</p></p>
