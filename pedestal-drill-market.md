<p><p>Pedestal drills are available in two main market types: floor pedestal drills and benchtop pedestal drills. Floor pedestal drills are larger, more powerful machines designed for heavy-duty industrial use, commonly found in workshops and factories. They are mounted on the floor for stability and have adjustable height settings. Benchtop pedestal drills, on the other hand, are smaller and more compact, suitable for light to medium-duty tasks in DIY and home workshops. They are tabletop machines that offer versatility and are easier to move around.</p></p>
