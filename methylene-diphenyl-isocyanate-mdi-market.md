<p><p>Methylene Diphenyl Isocyanate (MDI) is a versatile chemical compound used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics. There are two main types of MDI markets - Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI. Pure MDI is a high-grade, monomeric form of MDI used in applications requiring high performance and purity. Polymeric MDI, on the other hand, is a mixture of MDI isomers and higher functionality polymeric MDI used in applications where flexibility and durability are required. Both types play a crucial role in the production of polyurethane products.</p></p>
