<p><ul><li>PC Systems</li><li>Smart Camera Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision camera market can be broadly classified into two main types: PC Systems and Smart Camera Systems. </p><p>PC Systems are high-performance systems that are used for complex machine vision applications requiring advanced processing capabilities. They typically consist of a camera connected to a separate computer, which processes and analyzes the image data.</p><p>On the other hand, Smart Camera Systems are compact, self-contained units that integrate the camera, processor, and software into a single device. They are typically used for simpler applications that do not require as much processing power as PC Systems.</p></p>
