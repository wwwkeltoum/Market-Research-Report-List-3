<p><p>OTC tests market can be divided into two main types: Immunoassay and Lateral Flow Assay. Immunoassay tests involve the detection of antibodies or antigens in biological samples, typically using techniques like enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA). Lateral Flow Assay tests, on the other hand, involve the detection of a target analyte in a sample through the flow of liquid over a solid phase. Both types of tests are widely used for rapid and easy testing for various conditions, making them popular choices in the OTC market.</p></p>
