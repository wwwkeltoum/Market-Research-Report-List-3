HIS focuses on managing patient data and administrative functions in hospital settings. EMRS are digital versions of patients' paper charts. PACS enable the storage and retrieval of medical images. RIS focuses specifically on radiology departments. Other solutions include telemedicine platforms, health information exchanges, and telehealth services.</p></p>
