<p><p>The Fiber Laser Module for LiDAR market includes two main types: Long Pulsed and Short Pulsed. Long Pulsed lasers are characterized by emitting continuous pulses of laser light over a longer duration, allowing for better depth penetration in LiDAR applications. On the other hand, Short Pulsed lasers emit shorter pulses of laser light, enabling higher peak power and improved accuracy in detection and ranging. Both types of lasers play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of LiDAR systems.</p></p>
