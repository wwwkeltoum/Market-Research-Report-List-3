<p><ul><li>Single-frequency Bioimpedance Devicess</li><li>Multiple-frequency Bioimpedance Devicess</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless bioimpedance devices are categorized into two main types: single-frequency and multiple-frequency devices. Single-frequency bioimpedance devices use a single frequency of electrical current to measure impedance in the body, providing a basic assessment of body composition. On the other hand, multiple-frequency bioimpedance devices use varying frequencies to provide a more detailed analysis of body composition, including measurements of intracellular and extracellular water content. Both types of devices are used in healthcare, fitness, and research industries for monitoring and tracking changes in body composition.</p></p>
