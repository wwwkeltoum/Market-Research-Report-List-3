<p><p>Aircraft Carbon Braking System market includes two main types based on their operating mechanism: Pneumatical Type and Hydraulical Type braking systems. The Pneumatical Type system uses compressed air to actuate the brakes, offering precise and efficient control over braking. On the other hand, the Hydraulical Type system uses hydraulic fluid to transmit force to the brakes, providing a more responsive and powerful braking mechanism. Both types are essential in ensuring safe and reliable braking performance in aircraft operations.</p></p>
