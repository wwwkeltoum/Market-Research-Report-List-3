<p><p>In the Aluminium Holding Furnace market, there are three main types of furnaces: Pit Type, Tilting Type, and Bale-Out Type. Pit Type furnaces are typically installed below ground level for easy loading and unloading of the metal. Tilting Type furnaces allow for the molten metal to be easily poured out by tilting the furnace. Bale-Out Type furnaces have a door at the bottom for easy removal of solidified metal. These varying types cater to different preferences and requirements in the aluminum industry.</p></p>
