<p><p>The Medical Image sensor market consists of two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity to light, making them ideal for medical imaging applications that require precision and accuracy. On the other hand, CMOS sensors are more cost-effective and energy-efficient compared to CCD sensors, making them popular in portable medical devices. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in capturing and processing high-quality medical images for diagnostic and research purposes.</p></p>
