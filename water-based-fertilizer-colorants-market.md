<p><p>Water-based fertilizer colorants are available in two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble coloring agents that easily mix with water-based fertilizers, providing a transparent and vibrant color. On the other hand, pigments are insoluble particles that impart a more opaque and intense color to the fertilizer. Both types of colorants play a crucial role in enhancing the visual appeal of fertilizers, making them more attractive and easier to differentiate during application.</p></p>
