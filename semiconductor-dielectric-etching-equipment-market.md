<p><p>Semiconductor dielectric etching equipment includes both wet etching equipment and dry etching equipment. Wet etching equipment uses liquid chemicals to remove material from the semiconductor substrate, while dry etching equipment uses plasma or reactive gases for the same purpose. Wet etching is commonly used for simpler processes, while dry etching is preferred for more precise and complex etching requirements. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing industry by enabling the fabrication of intricate electronic devices.</p></p>
