<p><strong>The Cooled Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoconductive Detector (HgCdTe)</li><li>Photovoltaic Detector</li><li>Schottky Barrier Detector</li><li>Quantum Well Detector</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooled infrared detectors are used across various industries and applications, with different types catering to different needs. Photoconductive detectors, such as those made of HgCdTe, rely on changes in electrical conductivity to detect infrared radiation. Photovoltaic detectors convert photons directly into electrical current. Schottky barrier detectors use a metal-semiconductor junction to produce a photocurrent. Quantum well detectors utilize quantum wells to enhance the absorption of infrared radiation. Other types of cooled infrared detectors may include bolometers, pyroelectric detectors, and microbolometers.</p></p>
