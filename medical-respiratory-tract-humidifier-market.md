<p><strong>The Medical Respiratory Tract Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical respiratory tract humidifier market can be categorized into active type and passive type humidifiers. Active type humidifiers actively add moisture to the air by using a power source, such as electricity or a battery. These humidifiers usually have settings that allow for control over the amount of humidity delivered. In contrast, passive type humidifiers rely on natural processes, such as evaporation, to add moisture to the air. Passive humidifiers are typically simpler in design and do not require electricity to operate.</p></p>
