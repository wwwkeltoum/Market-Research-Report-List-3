<p><strong>The Fishmeal Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD)</li><li>Flame Dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fishmeal feed market types include Steam Dried (SD) and Flame Dried (FD). Steam Dried fishmeal is produced by cooking raw fish under high pressure and then pressing out the water content. This results in a high-quality protein feed. On the other hand, Flame Dried fishmeal is produced by cooking raw fish using direct heat from a flame. This process helps in preserving the nutritional value of the fishmeal by quickly drying it. Both SD and FD fishmeal are widely used in animal feed applications.</p></p>
