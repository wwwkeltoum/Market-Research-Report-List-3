<p><p>Non-PVC IV fluid bags come in two main types: single-chambered and multi-chambered. Single-chambered bags are commonly used for delivering a single type of fluid, while multi-chambered bags are used when different medications need to be administered simultaneously or sequentially. Both types offer advantages in terms of medication delivery and patient care.</p></p>
