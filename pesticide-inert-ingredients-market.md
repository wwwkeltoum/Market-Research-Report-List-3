<p><p>Pesticide inert ingredients can be classified into two main types: dry pesticide inert ingredients and liquid pesticide inert ingredients. Dry pesticide inert ingredients are solid in nature and are typically mixed with other pesticide active ingredients before application. On the other hand, liquid pesticide inert ingredients are in a liquid form and are often used in formulations such as emulsifiable concentrates or suspension concentrates. Both types of pesticide inert ingredients play a crucial role in improving the efficacy and stability of pesticide products.</p></p>
