<p><p>ICU patient monitor market can be categorized into high-acuity monitors, mid-acuity monitors, and low-acuity monitors based on the level of care they provide. High-acuity monitors are equipped with advanced features to monitor critical patients in intensive care units. Mid-acuity monitors are designed for patients requiring intermediate levels of monitoring, often found in step-down units. Low-acuity monitors are suitable for general ward patients who need basic vital sign monitoring. Each type of monitor caters to different levels of patient acuity and care requirements.</p></p>
