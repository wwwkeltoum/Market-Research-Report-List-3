<p><p>Electrostatic dehydrators come in three main types: AC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator, Combined AC-DC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator, and DC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator. The AC Field dehydrator uses alternating current to remove moisture from substances, while the Combined AC-DC Field dehydrator utilizes both types of currents for enhanced dehydration. On the other hand, the DC Field dehydrator solely relies on direct current for the dehydration process. Each type of dehydrator offers unique advantages depending on the specific application and requirements.</p></p>
