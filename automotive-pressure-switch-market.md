<p><p>Automotive pressure switches are essential components in vehicles that help monitor and control various systems. The Dual Function Pressure Switch is designed to detect high and low pressure levels in the system. High-Pressure Cut Out (HPCO) switches ensure safe operation by shutting down the system when pressure exceeds set limits. Fan switches control the operation of cooling fans to prevent overheating. Compressor Cycling switches regulate the cycling of the compressor to maintain optimal performance and efficiency in the system.</p></p>
