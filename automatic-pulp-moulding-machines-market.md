<p><ul><li>Rotary Pulp Moulding Machines</li><li>Reciprocating Pulp Moulding Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic pulp moulding machines are classified into two main types: rotary and reciprocating. Rotary pulp moulding machines have a rotating mold carousel, allowing for continuous production and high output capacity. Reciprocating pulp moulding machines have a moving mold system that goes back and forth, resulting in lower production speeds but greater flexibility in mold design. Both types of machines are widely used in the production of packaging materials, disposable tableware, and other molded pulp products.</p></p>
