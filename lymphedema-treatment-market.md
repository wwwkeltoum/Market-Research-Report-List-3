<p><p>Lymphedema treatment options mainly fall under three categories - surgical operation, compression therapy, and other interventions. Surgical operation involves procedures like lymphatic-venous anastomosis or lymph node transfer to improve lymphatic drainage. Compression therapy involves wearing specialized garments to reduce swelling and improve circulation. Other interventions may include manual lymph drainage, exercise therapy, and pneumatic compression devices. Each type of treatment aims to reduce swelling, manage symptoms, and improve the quality of life for individuals with lymphedema.</p></p>
