<p><p>Board mount humidity sensors are categorized into two types: Analog Output and Digital Output market. Analog Output sensors provide continuous readings through a voltage or current signal, allowing for real-time monitoring of humidity levels. Digital Output sensors convert humidity readings into digital data for easier interpretation and integration with microcontrollers or other digital devices. Both types offer advantages depending on the application, with Analog Output sensors being more suited for precise measurements and Digital Output sensors being more convenient for digital systems.</p></p>
