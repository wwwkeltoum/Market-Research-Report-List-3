<p><ul><li>Polyester-Based</li><li>Epoxy Resin-Based</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injectable adhesive anchors market is categorized into three major types: polyester-based, epoxy resin-based, and other. Polyester-based anchors offer high bond strength and are suitable for low to medium load applications. Epoxy resin-based anchors provide superior bonding performance and are commonly used in high load applications. The "other" category includes anchors made from various materials such as vinylester resins and acrylic adhesives. Each type of adhesive anchor offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
