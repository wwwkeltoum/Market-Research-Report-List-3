<p><p>Steering systems market includes Electronic Power Steering (EPS), which uses an electric motor to assist the driver in turning the wheel, providing better fuel efficiency and customization options. Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) combines hydraulic power with electronic control for smoother steering feel. Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) uses hydraulic pressure to assist the driver, providing reliable and consistent steering performance. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different customer preferences in terms of handling, efficiency, and maintenance.</p></p>
