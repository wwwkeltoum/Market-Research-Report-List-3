<p><strong>The Pipeline Basket Strainers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex Basket Strainers</li><li>Duplex Basket Strainers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipeline Basket Strainers are used to remove unwanted particles from a pipeline to protect downstream equipment. There are two main types of Pipeline Basket Strainers: Simplex Basket Strainers and Duplex Basket Strainers. Simplex Basket Strainers have a single straining element, making them ideal for applications where flow can be temporarily shut off for maintenance. Duplex Basket Strainers have two straining elements that can be switched out while the pipeline is still in operation, ensuring continuous filtration. Both types are essential for maintaining the efficiency and longevity of pipelines.</p></p>
