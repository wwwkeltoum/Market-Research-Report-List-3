<p><p>Animal prescription drugs market can be categorized into four types: oral type, smear type, injection type, and spray type. Oral type medications are given to animals via mouth, such as tablets or liquid medications. Smear type drugs are applied topically to the skin or fur of the animal. Injection type drugs are administered through injections. Spray type drugs are sprayed directly onto the animal's skin or fur. Each type serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the animal's condition and needs.</p></p>
