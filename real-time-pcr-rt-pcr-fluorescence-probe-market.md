Taqman probes are widely used for gene expression analysis due to their high specificity and sensitivity. Molecular beacons are hairpin-shaped probes that can distinguish between specific and non-specific amplicons. MGB-ODN probes contain minor groove binders for enhanced binding to DNA targets. These different types of probes cater to specific needs in the real-time PCR market, offering researchers a variety of options for their experiments.</p></p>
