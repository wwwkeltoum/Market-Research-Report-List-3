<p><p>Body Composition Analyzers Market is categorized into bio-impedance analyzers, dual-energy x-ray absorptiometry (DEXA), air displacement plethysmography, and others. Bio-impedance analyzers measure body composition by sending an electrical current through the body. DEXA uses x-rays to measure bone density and body composition. Air displacement plethysmography calculates body composition by measuring air displacement. Other types of body composition analyzers include ultrasound, infrared interactance, and skinfold calipers. These devices are used to assess body fat percentage, muscle mass, and overall health.</p></p>
