<p><strong>The Household Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure</li><li>Auto Continuous Positive Airway Pressure</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household ventilators are available in different types to cater to various respiratory conditions. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices deliver a constant flow of air to keep airways open during sleep. Auto CPAP adjusts air pressure based on the individual's breathing patterns. Other types of household ventilators may include bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) machines, which provide different pressures for inhalation and exhalation, and portable ventilators for on-the-go respiratory support. Each type of household ventilator serves a specific function in managing breathing difficulties.</p></p>
