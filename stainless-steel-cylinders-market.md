<p><p>Stainless steel cylinders are commonly classified into two main types: single-ended cylinders and double-ended cylinders. Single-ended cylinders have only one rod extending from one end of the cylinder, which is typically used for applications where a pushing force is required. Double-ended cylinders have rods extending from both ends of the cylinder, allowing for both pushing and pulling actions, making them ideal for applications requiring bi-directional movement. Both types offer durability, corrosion resistance, and high performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
