<p><p>Peristaltic pumps for the food and beverage market come in two main types: roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps use rotating rollers to compress the tubing and push the liquid forward, making them ideal for delicate food and beverage applications. Shoe type peristaltic pumps use a flexible shoe to squeeze the tubing and create flow, offering gentle and precise fluid handling for sensitive ingredients. Both types are widely used in the food and beverage industry for their hygienic and efficient pumping capabilities.</p></p>
