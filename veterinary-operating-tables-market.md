<p><p>Veterinary operating tables come in three main types: electric, hydraulic, and manual. Electric operating tables are powered by electricity and offer easy adjustability for height and positioning. Hydraulic tables use hydraulic fluid to raise, lower, and tilt the table to accommodate various procedures. Manual tables require physical adjustment by the user for positioning and height. Each type has its own set of benefits and drawbacks, catering to different preferences and needs of veterinarians and veterinary clinics.</p></p>
