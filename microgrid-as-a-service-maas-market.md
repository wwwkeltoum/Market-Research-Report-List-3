<p><p>Microgrid as a Service (MaaS) refers to the market types of Grid-Tied Microgrid and Independent Microgrid. A Grid-Tied Microgrid is connected to the main grid and can sell excess energy back to the grid. It is usually owned and operated by a utility company or a third-party provider. On the other hand, an Independent Microgrid operates autonomously without any connection to the main grid, relying solely on its own energy sources like solar panels or generators. Both market types offer unique benefits and opportunities for energy consumers looking for reliable and sustainable power solutions.</p></p>
