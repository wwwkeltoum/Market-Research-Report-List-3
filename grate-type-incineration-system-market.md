<p><p>Grate type incineration systems come in various types, including the reciprocating grate stoker, step grate stoker, and roller grate stoker. Reciprocating grate stokers use a back-and-forth motion to move waste materials through the combustion chamber. Step grate stokers have multiple sections that move waste materials from one step to the next. Roller grate stokers use rollers to move waste materials through the system. Each type of grate stoker offers unique benefits and is suited for different waste management needs.</p></p>
