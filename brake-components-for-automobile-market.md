<p><p>Brake components for automobiles include brake pads, brake shoes, brake lining, brake rotor, brake drum, and other related parts. Brake pads provide friction to slow down or stop a vehicle, while brake shoes press against the brake drum to create friction. Brake lining is used to protect the brake pads from wear and tear. Brake rotors are discs that are attached to the wheel and slow down the vehicle through friction. Brake drums are similar to rotors but have a different design. Other components in this market include brake calipers, brake fluids, and brake hoses.</p></p>
