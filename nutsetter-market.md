<p><p>Nutsetters are tools used to drive nuts into or out of surfaces. There are four main types: Hex Nutsetters, which are standard nut drivers with hexagonal-shaped sockets; Lobular Nutsetters, which have lobes inside the socket to grip the nut securely; Impact Nutsetters, designed for use with impact drivers to provide high torque; and Torsion Nutsetters, which have a torsion zone to absorb shock and prevent breakage. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences in the construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
