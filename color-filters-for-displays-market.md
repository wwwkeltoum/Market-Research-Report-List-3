<p><p>Color filters for displays come in two main types: RGB primary color filters and CMYG complementary color filters. RGB filters use red, green, and blue colors to produce a wide range of shades by combining these primary colors. On the other hand, CMYG filters use cyan, magenta, yellow, and green colors as complementary colors to achieve a similar effect. Both types of filters are used in displays to control the light that passes through and create accurate and vibrant color representations on screens.</p></p>
