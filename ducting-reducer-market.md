<p><p>Ducting reducers are used in HVAC systems to connect ducts of different sizes. Concentric reducers have a conical shape with both ends on the same axis, allowing for a smooth transition between duct sizes. Eccentric reducers have one end on a different axis than the other, making them suitable for applications where space is limited or flow direction needs to be changed. Both types of reducers play a crucial role in ensuring efficient airflow in ducting systems for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
