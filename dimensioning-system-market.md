<p><p>Dimensioning systems market can be categorized as Static and Dynamic markets based on their operating principles. Static dimensioning systems capture measurements of stationary objects, while dynamic systems capture measurements of moving objects in real-time. Static systems are commonly used in industries where objects are stationary, such as warehousing and logistics. Dynamic systems are more suitable for industries where objects are constantly moving, such as in conveyor belt systems or automated manufacturing processes. Both types of markets cater to different needs and applications within the industry.</p></p>
