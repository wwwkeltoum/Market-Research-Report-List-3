<p><p>Differential pressure meters are used to measure the pressure difference between two points in a system. There are three main types of differential pressure meters: piston style, diaphragm type, and bellows differential type. </p><p>Piston style meters use a movable piston to measure the pressure difference, while diaphragm type meters use a flexible diaphragm. Bellows differential type meters use a bellows system to measure pressure changes. Each type of meter has its own advantages and is used in various industries depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
