<p><p>Synthetic vascular grafts market types include ePTFE vascular graft, polyester vascular graft, PTFE vascular graft, and others. ePTFE vascular grafts are made from expanded polytetrafluoroethylene, known for its biocompatibility and durability. Polyester vascular grafts are made from woven or knitted polyester fabric, offering flexibility and strength. PTFE vascular grafts are made from polytetrafluoroethylene, providing excellent patency and resistance to thrombosis. Other types of synthetic vascular grafts may include materials such as polyurethane or silicone. Each type offers unique benefits for vascular surgery procedures.</p></p>
