<p><p>Turbidity analyzers are used to measure the cloudiness or haziness of a liquid. There are two main types of turbidity analyzer markets: dry media and wet media. Dry media analyzers use a light beam to detect particles in the liquid, while wet media analyzers use a sensor submerged in the liquid. Dry media analyzers are commonly used for clean water applications, while wet media analyzers are more suitable for dirty or abrasive liquids. Both types of analyzers provide accurate and reliable measurements of turbidity in various industrial processes.</p></p>
