<p><p>Landscape construction services encompass two main market types: hardscape and softscape. This type of market focuses on the more permanent features of a landscape. On the other hand, softscape involves the use of living elements like plants, trees, and flowers to enhance the natural beauty of a landscape. Softscape market focuses on the more organic and fluid aspects of landscaping design.</p></p>
