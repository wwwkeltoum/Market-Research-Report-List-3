<p><p>Nano-SiO2 market can be classified into two main types: Dry Method and Wet Method. Dry Method involves the production of nano-SiO2 through processes such as gas-phase and solid-phase methods, resulting in powders or particles. On the other hand, Wet Method involves the synthesis of nano-SiO2 through liquid-phase processes, producing suspensions or colloids. Each method has its own benefits and applications, with Dry Method being preferred for certain industries like electronics, while Wet Method is more commonly used in industries like coatings and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
