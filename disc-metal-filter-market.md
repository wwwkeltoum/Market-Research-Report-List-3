<p><p>Disc metal filters are available in two main types: wire mesh and laser drilled holes. Wire mesh filters consist of a woven metal mesh that traps particles as liquid or gas flows through it. On the other hand, filters with laser drilled holes have precise, uniform holes created using laser technology to accurately control the filtration process. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications to remove impurities and contaminants from fluids and gases.</p></p>
