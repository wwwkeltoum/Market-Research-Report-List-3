<p><p>ENT operating tables are used in surgery for ear, nose, and throat procedures. There are three main types of ENT operating tables: electric, hydraulic, and manual. Electric operating tables allow for precise adjustments using a motorized system. Hydraulic operating tables use fluid power to adjust height and positioning. Manual operating tables require manual adjustment by the surgical team. Each type offers different capabilities and benefits, catering to the specific needs and preferences of healthcare providers and patients in the ENT field.</p></p>
