<p><p>The automotive differential gear market consists of various types including planetary, rack and pinion, worm, helical, non-metallic, and others. Planetary differentials use a set of gears to distribute power evenly, while rack and pinion differentials utilize a straight-toothed gear mechanism. Worm differentials have a spiral gear design for high torque applications, and helical differentials have angled teeth for smoother operation. Non-metallic differentials use materials like carbon fiber for lightness and durability. Other differential types may include variations or combinations of these designs for specific automotive applications.</p></p>
