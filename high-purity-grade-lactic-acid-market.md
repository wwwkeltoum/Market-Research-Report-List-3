<p><p>High purity grade lactic acid is available in three main types: L-Lactic Acid, D-Lactic Acid, and DL-Lactic Acid. L-Lactic Acid is the naturally occurring form found in food and the human body. D-Lactic Acid is the mirror image of L-Lactic Acid and is typically produced synthetically. DL-Lactic Acid is a mixture of both L- and D-Lactic Acid forms. Each type has specific applications in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics, depending on their unique properties and benefits.</p></p>
