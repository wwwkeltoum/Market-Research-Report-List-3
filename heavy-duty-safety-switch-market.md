<p><strong>The Heavy Duty Safety Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fusible Disconnect</li><li>Non-fusible Disconnect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heavy Duty Safety Switch market consists of two main types: Fusible Disconnect and Non-fusible Disconnect. Fusible Disconnect switches have fuses that can be replaced, providing protection against overloading and short circuits. Non-fusible Disconnect switches do not have fuses and rely on a solid blade or a similar mechanism for disconnection. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings to provide a safe and reliable way to disconnect power to equipment for maintenance or emergency situations.</p></p>
