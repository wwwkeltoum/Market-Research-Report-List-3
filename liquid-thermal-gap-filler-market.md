<p><p>Liquid thermal gap fillers are materials used to improve thermal conductivity in electronic devices by filling gaps between components. Silicone liquid thermal gap fillers are made from silicone material, offering high thermal conductivity and stability. Non-silicone liquid thermal gap fillers, on the other hand, are made from materials other than silicone, providing different thermal properties. Both types of liquid thermal gap fillers play a crucial role in managing heat dissipation in electronic devices, but their composition and properties vary based on the material used.</p></p>
