<p><p>Sheet based waterproofing membranes are used to protect buildings from water ingress and are available in various types. Bituminous membranes are made from bitumen and provide excellent waterproofing properties. PVC membranes are made from polyvinyl chloride and are known for their durability and flexibility. EPDM membranes are made from ethylene propylene diene monomer and offer good resistance to UV rays. Other types of membranes may include TPO (thermoplastic olefin) and HDPE (high-density polyethylene) which also provide effective waterproofing solutions.</p></p>
