<p><p>Electrical cable conduits are used to protect and route electrical wiring in various applications. There are two main types of conduits: flexible and rigid. Flexible conduits are made of materials like PVC or metal and are typically used in applications where the wiring needs to bend or move. Rigid conduits, on the other hand, are made of materials like steel or aluminum and are used in applications where the wiring needs more protection and stability. Both types of conduits play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of electrical systems.</p></p>
