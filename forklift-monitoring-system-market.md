<p><p>Forklift monitoring systems include speed monitoring, overload protection, efficiency monitoring, and security monitoring. Speed monitoring ensures that forklifts are operating at safe speeds, preventing accidents and damage to goods. Overload protection prevents forklifts from lifting loads that exceed their capacity, reducing the risk of accidents and equipment damage. Efficiency monitoring tracks fuel consumption, maintenance schedules, and operator performance to optimize productivity. Security monitoring includes features like geofencing and tracking to prevent theft and unauthorized use of forklifts.</p></p>
