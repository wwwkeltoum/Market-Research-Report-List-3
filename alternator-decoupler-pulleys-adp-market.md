<p><strong>The Alternator Decoupler Pulleys (ADP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overrunning Alternator Decoupler (OAD) Pulleys</li><li>One-Way Clutch (OWC) Pulleys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alternator Decoupler Pulleys (ADP) are used in vehicles to reduce vibrations and noise, while also improving fuel efficiency and overall engine performance. There are two main types of ADPs on the market, including Overrunning Alternator Decoupler (OAD) Pulleys and One-Way Clutch (OWC) Pulleys. OAD pulleys allow the alternator to spin freely in one direction, reducing strain on the engine during acceleration and deceleration. OWC pulleys, on the other hand, only allow the alternator to spin in one direction, preventing engine damage and improving overall efficiency.</p></p>
