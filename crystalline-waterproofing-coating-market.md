The two main types of these coatings are cement-based permeable crystalline waterproof coatings and water-based permeable crystalline waterproof coatings. Cement-based coatings use crystalline technology to fill and seal cracks in concrete, while water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to apply. Both types effectively protect structures from water damage and are commonly used in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
