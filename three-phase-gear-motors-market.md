<p><ul><li>Solid-shaft Gear Motor</li><li>Hollow-shaft Gear Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid-shaft gear motors are designed with a solid output shaft that directly transmits torque to the driven equipment. They are commonly used in applications where the load is relatively constant. In contrast, hollow-shaft gear motors have a hollow output shaft that allows for more flexibility in connecting to the driven equipment. They are ideal for applications where there is a need to easily adjust the position or length of the output shaft. Both types of gear motors are widely used in various industries for their efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
