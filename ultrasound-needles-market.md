<p><p>Ultrasound needles are used in various medical procedures to obtain tissue or fluid samples for diagnostic purposes. Aspiration needles, also known as EUS-FNA needles, are used to collect fluid samples by suction, while biopsy needles, or EUS-FNB needles, are used to acquire tissue samples for analysis. Both types of needles are essential in procedures such as endoscopic ultrasound-guided fine needle aspiration (EUS-FNA) and endoscopic ultrasound-guided fine needle biopsy (EUS-FNB) to aid in the diagnosis of various medical conditions.</p></p>
