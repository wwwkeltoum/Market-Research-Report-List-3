<p><p>Safety Instrumented System (SIS) market is divided into three main types - sensor, logic solver, and final element. The sensor market consists of devices that detect process parameters, such as pressure or temperature. Logic solver market includes programmable devices that analyze sensor data and make decisions to take corrective actions. Final element market involves devices that physically act on the process, such as valves or actuators. These three types work together to ensure the safety and reliability of industrial processes.</p></p>
