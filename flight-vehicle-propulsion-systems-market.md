<p><strong>The Flight Vehicle Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Reaction Propulsion System</li><li>Indirect Reaction Propulsion System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct reaction propulsion systems involve the direct ejection of mass to produce thrust, commonly seen in rockets and jet propulsion engines. Indirect reaction propulsion systems, on the other hand, work by generating thrust through the production of a working fluid or propellant to produce a reaction force, such as in a gas turbine engine or propeller-driven aircraft. Both systems are crucial in the field of flight vehicle propulsion, each offering unique advantages and applications for various types of aircraft.</p></p>
