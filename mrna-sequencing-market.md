There are three main types of mRNA sequencing markets: PCR, Microarray, and Sequencing. PCR-based methods amplify specific regions of RNA for analysis. Microarray technology involves immobilizing probes on a solid surface to detect gene expression levels. Sequencing directly reads the nucleotide sequence of RNA molecules, offering high-throughput and comprehensive gene expression analysis. Each market type has its own advantages and applications in mRNA sequencing studies.</p></p>
