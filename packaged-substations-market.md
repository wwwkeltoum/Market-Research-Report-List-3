<p><p>Packaged substations are available in two main types: General Type and Compact Type. On the other hand, Compact Type packaged substations are smaller in size and are designed for limited space applications such as urban areas or residential neighborhoods. These substations are more space-efficient and easier to install in areas with space constraints.</p></p>
