<p><strong>The Preclinical MRI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment</li><li>Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Preclinical MRI equipment includes two main types: Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment and Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment. Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment uses a fixed magnetic field generated by permanent magnets, making it cost-effective and easier to maintain. On the other hand, Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment utilizes superconducting coils cooled to very low temperatures, offering higher magnetic field strength and better image quality. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various preclinical research applications.</p></p>
