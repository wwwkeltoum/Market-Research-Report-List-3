<p><p>Automotive Engine Management Systems include Multipoint Fuel Injection (MPFi) Systems, Gasoline Direct Injection (GDi) Systems, Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDi) Systems, and Other types. MPFi Systems deliver fuel to the engine cylinders individually for improved efficiency. GDi Systems spray fuel directly into the combustion chamber for increased power and fuel economy. CRDi Systems use a common rail to deliver fuel at high pressure for better performance. Other systems may include Variable Valve Timing (VVT) and electronic throttle control for optimal engine operation.</p></p>
