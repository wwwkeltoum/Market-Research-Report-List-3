<p><p>PC-TV tuners come in two main types: USB and TapC. USB TV tuners are compact devices that can be easily plugged into a computer's USB port, allowing users to watch and record TV programs on their computer. TapC TV tuners, on the other hand, are devices that are typically integrated into a computer's motherboard, providing users with the ability to watch and record TV programs directly on their computer without the need for an external device. Both types offer similar functionality, but differ in terms of form factor and connectivity.</p></p>
