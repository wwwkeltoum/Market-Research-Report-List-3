<p><p>On-Grid PV Power Station refers to a charging station that is connected to the main electricity grid, allowing excess energy produced by the solar panels to be fed back into the grid for compensation or credit. Off-Grid PV Power Station, on the other hand, operates independently of the main grid, storing excess energy in batteries for later use when there is low or no sunlight. Both types of PV charging stations play a crucial role in harnessing solar energy for sustainable power generation.</p></p>
