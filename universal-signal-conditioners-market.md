<p><p>Universal signal conditioners are electronic devices used to process and convert signals from sensors or other devices. The market for these devices can be categorized into single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel types. Single-channel conditioners process signals from one sensor or source, dual-channel conditioners handle signals from two sources simultaneously, and multi-channel conditioners are capable of processing signals from multiple sources at once. Each type offers different capabilities depending on the number of channels required for signal processing.</p></p>
