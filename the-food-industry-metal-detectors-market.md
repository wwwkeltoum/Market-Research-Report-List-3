<p><p>The food industry metal detectors market includes two main types: Balanced Coil systems and Ferrous-in-Foil systems. Balanced Coil systems use two coils to detect metal contaminants in food products, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Ferrous-in-Foil systems specifically target ferrous metals that are concealed within foil packaging, providing effective detection capabilities for this type of packaging. Both types of metal detectors play a critical role in ensuring food safety and quality control in the food industry.</p></p>
