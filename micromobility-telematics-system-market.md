<p><p>Micromobility telematics systems can be classified into three market types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated. </p><p>Embedded systems are built directly into the micromobility device, providing real-time data without the need for external connections. </p><p>Tethered systems rely on a separate device, like a smartphone, to gather and transmit data. </p><p>Integrated systems combine both embedded and tethered functionalities, offering a comprehensive solution that seamlessly integrates with the micromobility device. Each market type offers unique benefits and caters to different user preferences.</p></p>
