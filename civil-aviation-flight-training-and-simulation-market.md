<p><ul><li>Full Flight Simulators (FFS)</li><li>Flight Training Devices (FTD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Civil Aviation Flight Training and Simulation market includes two main types of simulators: Full Flight Simulators (FFS) and Flight Training Devices (FTD). FFS are highly advanced simulators that offer a full cockpit replica and realistic flight experience. They are used for type rating and recurrent training for commercial pilots. FTDs are less complex but still provide a realistic cockpit environment for training purposes. They are used for procedural training and practice of specific maneuvers. Both simulators play a crucial role in training pilots and ensuring safety in the aviation industry.</p></p>
