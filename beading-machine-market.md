<p><p>The beading machine market is categorized into three main types: manual, electric, and others. Manual beading machines require manually turning a crank or handle to create beads on metal sheets. Electric beading machines use electricity to automate the bead rolling process, making it easier and faster. The "others" category includes different types of beading machines that do not fit into the manual or electric categories, such as hydraulic or pneumatic machines. Each type offers its own advantages and is used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
