<p><p>The Smart Card OEM/ODM market includes two major types: One-Time Password (OTP) and Multifactor Authentication (MFA). OTP systems generate a unique password for each login attempt, enhancing security by minimizing the risk of password theft. MFA solutions combine multiple authentication factors, such as passwords, biometrics, and tokens, to provide stronger security measures. Both OTP and MFA technologies are crucial in safeguarding sensitive information and preventing unauthorized access to systems, making them essential components in the smart card industry.</p></p>
