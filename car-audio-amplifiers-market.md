<p><p>Car audio amplifiers can be classified into two main market types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and After Market. OEM amplifiers are typically installed by the car manufacturer and are integrated into the vehicle's audio system from the factory. After Market amplifiers, on the other hand, are purchased separately by consumers and installed aftermarket to upgrade or enhance the car's audio system. Both market types offer a range of options for consumers to customize their car's audio setup according to their preferences and requirements.</p></p>
