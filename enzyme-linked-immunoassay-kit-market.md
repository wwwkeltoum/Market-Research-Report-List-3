<p><p>Enzyme-linked Immunoassay (ELISA) Kits are categorized into different types based on the method used to detect and quantify the target antigen. Sandwich ELISA involves capturing the antigen between two antibodies. Indirect ELISA detects antibodies by using a labeled secondary antibody. Competitive ELISA quantifies antigens by competition with a labeled antigen. Other types of ELISA include direct ELISA, which directly detects the antigen, and inhibition ELISA, which measures the inhibition of antigen-antibody binding. Each type has specific applications and advantages in the market.</p></p>
