<p><p>Surface modifier/modification agents are divided into four main types - coupling agent, surfactant, organic polymer surface treatment agent, and inorganic modifier. Coupling agents are used to improve adhesion between dissimilar materials. Surfactants reduce surface tension and enhance wetting. Organic polymer surface treatment agents provide protection and improve surface properties. Inorganic modifiers are used to enhance properties such as hardness, strength, and thermal stability. These agents play a crucial role in modifying and enhancing the performance of various materials in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
