<p><p>Fire sprinkler systems are crucial for fire protection in buildings and come in three main types: dry, wet, and deluge sprinkler systems. Dry sprinkler systems contain pressurized air or nitrogen until activated by heat, wet systems have water constantly in the pipes ready to flow upon detection of fire, and deluge systems release a large volume of water simultaneously over a whole area once a fire alarm is triggered. Each system has its unique applications and benefits for fire suppression.</p></p>
