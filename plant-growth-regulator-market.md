<p><ul><li>Cytokinins</li><li>Auxins</li><li>Gibberellins</li><li>Ethylene/Ethylene Releasers</li><li>Mepiquat Chloride</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plant Growth Regulators can be classified into five main types: Cytokinins, Auxins, Gibberellins, Ethylene/Ethylene Releasers, and Mepiquat Chloride. Cytokinins promote cell division and overall plant growth. Auxins regulate plant development and growth by promoting cell elongation. Gibberellins stimulate stem elongation and seed germination. Ethylene and Ethylene releasers influence fruit ripening and leaf shedding. Mepiquat Chloride is used to regulate plant height by inhibiting gibberellin synthesis. Each type of plant growth regulator plays a vital role in the development and growth of plants.</p></p>
