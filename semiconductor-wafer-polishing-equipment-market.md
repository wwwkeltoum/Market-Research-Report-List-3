<p><p>Semiconductor wafer polishing equipment market can be categorized into three main types: mechanical polishing, ultrasonic polishing, and other market. Mechanical polishing involves the use of abrasive materials to remove surface imperfections from semiconductor wafers. Ultrasonic polishing utilizes ultrasonic vibrations to achieve a higher level of precision and uniformity in polishing. The other market consists of various innovative polishing technologies such as chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) and electropolishing, which offer unique advantages in terms of efficiency and quality of the polishing process.</p></p>
