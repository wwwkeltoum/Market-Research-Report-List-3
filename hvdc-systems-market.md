<p><ul><li>Point-to-Point Transmission</li><li>Back-to-Back Station</li><li>Multi-Terminal System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVDC Systems Market includes three main types of systems: Point-to-Point Transmission, Back-to-Back Station, and Multi-Terminal System. Point-to-Point Transmission involves connecting two specific points with long-distance transmission lines. Back-to-Back Station involves connecting two AC systems with a HVDC link to synchronize the systems. Multi-Terminal System involves connecting multiple AC systems to a common DC link, allowing power transfer between different points simultaneously. Each market type caters to specific transmission needs and offers different benefits in terms of efficiency and flexibility.</p></p>
