<p><p>Box and Carton Overwrapping Machines come in three main types based on their level of automation: Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Automatic. Manual machines require human intervention for each step of the overwrapping process, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Semi-Automatic machines automate some aspects of the process, such as sealing and cutting, but still require manual loading of the boxes. Automatic machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention and increasing efficiency for large-scale productions. Each type caters to different production needs and scales.</p></p>
