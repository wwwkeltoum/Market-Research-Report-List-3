<p><p>Portable soil field testing equipment market includes various types of equipment used for different soil testing procedures such as soil sampling tests, triaxial tests, CBR tests, density & consolidation tests, and water permeability tests. Soil sampling tests are used to collect soil samples, while triaxial tests measure soil strength. CBR tests determine the bearing capacity of soil, density & consolidation tests measure soil compaction, and water permeability tests assess how water flows through the soil. These equipment are essential for on-site soil testing in construction and environmental applications.</p></p>
