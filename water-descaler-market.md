<p><p>The Water Descaler market is divided into two main types - Electromagnetic Type and Capacitive Electronic Type. Electromagnetic Type water descalers use electromagnetic pulses to alter the structure of minerals in water, preventing scale buildup. On the other hand, Capacitive Electronic Type descalers use electrical impulses to prevent scale formation. Both types are effective in reducing scale buildup in water systems, but the choice between the two depends on specific water quality issues and user preferences.</p></p>
