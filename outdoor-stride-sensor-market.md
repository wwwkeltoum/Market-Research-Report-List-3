<p><ul><li>Gravity Induced</li><li>Vibration Induced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Outdoor Stride Sensor market types include Gravity Induced and Vibration Induced sensors. Gravity Induced sensors measure the movement of the body by detecting changes in gravitational force, providing accurate data on steps taken and distance covered. On the other hand, Vibration Induced sensors use vibrations caused by foot strikes to track movement, making them ideal for outdoor activities where GPS signal may be weak. Both types offer reliable tracking of stride and running metrics for outdoor fitness enthusiasts.</p></p>
