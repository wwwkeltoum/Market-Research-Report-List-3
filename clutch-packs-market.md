<p><strong>The Clutch Packs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disc</li><li>Multi Disc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single disc clutch packs consist of only one disc and pressure plate, providing a simpler design and lower cost. They are commonly used in smaller vehicles with lower torque requirements. Multi disc clutch packs contain multiple discs and pressure plates stacked together, allowing for higher torque capacity and smoother engagement. They are typically used in high-performance vehicles or those with heavier loads. Both types serve the same purpose of transmitting power from the engine to the transmission by engaging and disengaging the clutch as needed.</p></p>
