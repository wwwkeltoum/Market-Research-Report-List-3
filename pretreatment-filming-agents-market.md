<p><p>Pretreatment filming agents are used to prevent corrosion on metal surfaces. There are three main types of pretreatment filming agents based on their composition: inorganic anti-corrosion agents, organic anti-corrosion agents, and polymer anti-corrosion agents. Inorganic agents work by forming a protective barrier on the metal surface, while organic agents contain organic compounds that inhibit corrosion. Polymer agents are made up of polymers that adhere to the metal surface and provide a protective coating against corrosion. Each type has its own unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
