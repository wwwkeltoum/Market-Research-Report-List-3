<p><strong>The Grounding Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grounding pads are medical devices used during electrosurgical procedures to prevent burns and shocks by directing electrical current away from the patient's body. There are two main types of grounding pads available in the market - monopolar and bipolar. Monopolar grounding pads have a single electrode and are used in conjunction with a dispersive electrode, while bipolar grounding pads have two electrodes placed close together on the same pad. Both types serve the same purpose of safely dissipating electrical current during surgery.</p></p>
