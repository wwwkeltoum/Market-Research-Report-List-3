<p><p>Food deaerators market includes various types such as Spray-Tray Type Deaerators, Spray Type Deaerators, and Vacuum Type Deaerators. Spray-Tray Type Deaerators involve spraying steam into a chamber while also using trays to increase air removal. Spray Type Deaerators use high-pressure steam to remove dissolved gases. Vacuum Type Deaerators operate by creating a vacuum in a closed tank to remove dissolved gases. Each type of deaerator offers different benefits and is used in various food processing applications.</p></p>
