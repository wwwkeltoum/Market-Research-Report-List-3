<p><p>Production check weighers come in two main market types: In-motion check weighers and Intermittent check weighers. In-motion check weighers are designed to continuously weigh products as they move along a conveyor belt, allowing for high-speed and efficient sorting of products based on weight. On the other hand, Intermittent check weighers are used for manual or semi-automatic weight checks of products that are not continuously moving, making them ideal for smaller scale operations or batch processing. Both types of check weighers play a crucial role in ensuring product quality and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
