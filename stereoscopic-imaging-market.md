<p><p>Stereoscopic imaging refers to the technology that creates a sense of depth in images or videos. There are two main types of stereoscopic imaging markets: lens stereoscope and mirror stereoscope. Lens stereoscopes use specially designed lenses to create the illusion of depth, while mirror stereoscopes use mirrors to achieve the same effect. Both technologies are commonly used in virtual reality headsets, 3D movies, and other applications where a realistic sense of depth is desired.</p></p>
