<p><p>Real-Time Location System (RTLS) market types include RFID, Wi-Fi, Ultrasound, Infrared, ZigBee, Ultra-Wide Band (UWB), and others. RFID technology uses radio waves to track objects, while Wi-Fi utilizes wireless networks for location tracking. Ultrasound systems use sound waves, Infrared technology uses infrared light, ZigBee relies on low-power wireless communication, and UWB systems use short-range radio waves. Other RTLS technologies may include Bluetooth, GPS, or magnetic field-based systems for precise real-time location tracking in various industries.</p></p>
