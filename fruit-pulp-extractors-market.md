<p><p>Fruit pulp extractors come in two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic extractors require some manual input from operators, such as feeding fruits into the machine and monitoring the extraction process. On the other hand, fully automatic extractors are more advanced and require minimal to no manual intervention, as they are equipped with sensors and automated features. Both types cater to different market needs, with semi-automatic models being more affordable and suitable for small-scale operations, while fully automatic models are ideal for larger-scale production.</p></p>
