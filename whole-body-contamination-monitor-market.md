<p><p>The Whole Body Contamination Monitor Market consists of two main types of detectors: Plastic Scintillation Detector and Gas Flow Proportional Detector. Plastic Scintillation Detector works by measuring the amount of light emitted when radiation interacts with plastic scintillator material. Gas Flow Proportional Detector, on the other hand, measures ionization in a gas-filled chamber when radiation passes through it. Both types of detectors are used in radiation monitoring to ensure the safety of individuals who may have been exposed to radioactive contamination.</p></p>
