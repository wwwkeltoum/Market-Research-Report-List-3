<p><p>Veterinary ultrasound imaging market consists of various types of systems, including 2D ultrasound imaging systems, Doppler ultrasound, and 3D or 4D ultrasound imaging systems. 2D ultrasound systems provide detailed black and white images of the animal's internal organs. Doppler ultrasound is used to measure blood flow and detect any abnormalities. 3D or 4D ultrasound systems provide a more comprehensive view of the animal's anatomy, allowing for better visualization and diagnosis. These different types of ultrasound systems cater to specific needs and preferences of veterinary practitioners.</p></p>
